WHO

Everyone who owns/uses a phone, tablet, laptop, or any device with wifi needs to watch this video and do as many steps as they can. These days people try to rob your identity online more than they rob you in person. Thieves are only getting better at what they do and the internet users are getting worst at protecting themselves.

WHY

Eveyrthing that we do online is recorded somewhere and reviewed by computers and anylists to try to learn what you want and use it for advertisement. As harmless as that sounds, it is an invasion of your privacy, but it is very much legal. WE give them permission to do whatever they want with the data collected from OUR devices. When you set up a new device for instance, or create a new account, no one ever stops to read the fine print. They flat out say that they are going to sell your information and use it and we agree to it without even looking.

HOW

This video along with my notes and additions will give you steps on what you need to do in order to stay safe on the internet. You don't need to dig around for different people saying different things on how to keep yourself safe. This video has sources and links (which I provided) that show how reliable and trusted this information is.
